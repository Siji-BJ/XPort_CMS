IDP Program

Our Maritime Centre of Excellence has been studying a variety of PCS initiatives from around the world and is trying to analyze the cause for the inertia with the vast majority of cases and found that individualized approaches need to be taken to make it work and achieve the desired benefits. Being a true multi-stakeholder enterprise that ports are, we also believe that customers need to be partners in product development, implementation & transition management and recognize that through a formal program called IDP.

At xPort, we are trying to create the most effective platform for MSW and hence use the most modern IT tools to achieve results. But we recognize that individual port processes and the interplay of those with the local stakeholders might need consideration in the process. Hence, we include the ports as partners in our product roadmap and testing program. And we leverage ports from diverse geographical regions that have the maturity to partner us to be able to define and validate the best practices in various processes.
